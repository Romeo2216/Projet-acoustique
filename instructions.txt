You will hear 3 sounds (A, B and X).

A and B are different but X can be either A or B.

Your goal is to find whether X is A or B.

If you are not sure of the right answer, press randomly one of the two answers.

You can listen to those sounds as many times as you wish within 30 seconds.

This test takes between 15 to 30 minutes to complete.

When you are ready to begin, please click on the 'Ready' button.
